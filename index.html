Bob 画图变音乐 Doodle to Sound

Select a color, adjust brush size, and draw on the canvas. Then, click "Turn Drawing Into Sound" to hear your creation! Higher notes are at the top, lower notes at the bottom. Drawing plays from left to right. Different colors have different timbres. 选择颜色、调整笔刷大小，然后在画布上绘制。 接着点击“将绘画转为声音”，即可聆听你的创作！ 顶部对应高音，底部对应低音。 绘画从左向右播放，不同颜色拥有不同音色。
